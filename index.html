About Me

My name is Coral Yu and I am a sophomore in high school. I go to Summit Shasta and play basketball for the school team. I also do another sport called dragon boat for another high school team outside of school. I don't only play for the school team, but I also play in two different club teams outside of school.

I play basketball on a total of three different teams including the high school varsity girls basketball team. I play basketball for fun and because of the competiveness it gives to me that I enjoy. It is also considered one of my favorite sports that I play. This is also a sport that requires me to travel every once in a while because of the places where the tournaments are at. I've been to Las Vegas, Reno, Sacramento, and Los Angeles.

Another hobby I have is dragon boat which is a competive sport where a lot of people paddle on one boat together really fast and are timed and race against other teams. It is a very competive sport that I've started participating in last year and even though I don't go to many places unlike basketball, I still get to experience and participate in races that are at nearby places like Oakland or Alameda.
